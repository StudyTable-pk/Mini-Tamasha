Season 1 Auditions LIVE!

Welcome to Mini Tamasha, a fan-made reality show inspired by your favorite big brother style programs. This is an unofficial production and has no direct link or affiliation with any official Tamasha broadcasts or entities.

Are you ready to enter the house, face challenges, and become the ultimate Mini Tamasha champion? Register now for your chance to be part of Season 1!

Mini Tamasha will be played entirely online across various platforms, ensuring a wide reach and interactive experience. We prioritize contestant privacy; no real identities will be revealed. Each participant will be assigned a unique contestant name, inspired by past Tamasha seasons, such as Umar Aalam, Arooba Mirza, Malik Aqeel, Arslan, Dania Enwar, Saima Baloch, and many more. Get ready to play a new character and make your mark!

Participation in Mini Tamasha is completely free! There are no fees or charges to apply or to be a contestant. Applicants must be 15 years of age or older to be eligible.

About the Show

  • Mini Tamasha is a fan-made online reality show inspired by Tamasha.
  • 15 contestants will be selected.
  • Each contestant will be given the name of a past Tamasha player (no real identity revealed).
  • The show will run online for 5 weeks on Discord.
  • There is no entry fee.
  • The winner will be featured on social media (no financial or physical prize).
  • To participate, age must be 15+.

Rules

  1. No abusive language.
  2. No personal attacks on real life or identity.
  3. Contestants cannot DM other contestants without permission.
  4. Alliances are allowed, but betrayal is part of the game.
  5. Cheating in tasks will lead to nomination. Repeated cheating can lead to eviction.
  6. A captain will be chosen every 3rd day (by task or voting). The captain is safe for 3 days.
  7. If a contestant is absent during a task, they will be directly nominated.
  8. Nominations may be secret or open.
  9. Every nominated contestant will get a voting link which they can share publicly. The average of house votes + audience votes will decide elimination. In case of tie, audience votes will decide.
